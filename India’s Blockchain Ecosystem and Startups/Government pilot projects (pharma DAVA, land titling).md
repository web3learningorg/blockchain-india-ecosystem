# How Blockchain is Transforming Banking

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing the $31 trillion trade finance and banking industry. Experts predict tokenized trade finance could reach **$4.8 trillion by 2034**, with real-world adoption already underway—JP Morgan's Onyx platform has handled **$300 billion** in intraday repo transactions.

## Key Applications of Blockchain in Banking

### 1. Identity Systems
- **HSBC Digital Vault**: Streamlines KYC and asset management, managing **$13.5B** in private assets.  
- **JP Morgan Quorum**: Uses zero-knowledge proofs for privacy-preserving identity verification.  
- **Self-Sovereign Identity**: Empowers customers to control digital identities via cryptographic keys.

### 2. Crypto Custody
- **BNY Mellon Digital Asset Platform**: Custody for Bitcoin, Ethereum alongside traditional investments.  
- **Standard Chartered's Zodia Custody**: Provides secure, compliant, and insured institutional crypto storage.

### 3. Cross-Border Payments
- **JP Morgan Liink**: Connects 400+ banks for faster, transparent interbank communication.  
- **RippleNet**: Real-time global payments network with 100+ financial institutions.  
- **Visa B2B Connect**: Streamlines high-value international transactions using blockchain.

### 4. Trade Finance
- **We.trade**: European blockchain platform enabling SMEs to manage international trade efficiently.  
- **Marco Polo Network**: Standardizes data across banks and corporates, accelerating trade finance.

### 5. Asset Tokenization
- **Goldman Sachs**: Conducted first tokenized asset repo transaction.  
- **UBS Tokenized Fund Initiatives**: Pilot blockchain-based investment funds for improved transparency and efficiency.

## Conclusion
Blockchain is transforming banking with secure identity verification, efficient payments, digitized trade finance, and innovative tokenized financial products. With tokenized trade finance projected to reach nearly **$5 trillion by 2034**, major banks are investing heavily in decentralized solutions, reshaping the global financial ecosystem.
