# How Blockchain is Revolutionizing Banking

Blockchain is transforming the $31 trillion trade finance and banking industry. Tokenized trade finance is projected to reach **$4.8 trillion by 2034**, with real-world adoption already happening—JP Morgan's Onyx has processed **$300 billion** in intraday repo transactions.

## Key Innovations in Banking

- **Identity Systems**: HSBC's Digital Vault and JP Morgan Quorum streamline KYC and secure identity verification, while self-sovereign identity gives users control over their data.  
- **Crypto Custody**: Platforms like BNY Mellon and Standard Chartered's Zodia Custody securely manage digital assets for institutional clients.  
- **Cross-Border Payments**: RippleNet, JP Morgan Liink, and Visa B2B Connect enable faster, cheaper, and transparent global transactions.  
- **Trade Finance**: We.trade and Marco Polo Network digitize trade processes, reducing delays and fraud.  
- **Asset Tokenization**: Goldman Sachs and UBS are creating tokenized financial products, enhancing liquidity and investment opportunities.

Blockchain is making banking more **secure, transparent, and efficient**, ushering in a new era of global finance.
